Overview

Dorothy's Job is an isometric twin-stick shooter with procedural levels. The project focuses on high-speed combat and a unique corporate-dystopian aesthetic.

The Challenge

Creating a balanced procedural system that maintains narrative pacing was our biggest hurdle. We developed a "Room Weight" algorithm to ensure high-intensity encounters were spaced correctly.
